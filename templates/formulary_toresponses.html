Multidimensional Assessment of Research in Context (MARC) Tool

Below you find some short questions regarding your research project. After submitting the form, a summary with your answers and descriptions accompanied by a compass plot will appear. The graph indicates the match of your research idea with three categories that are controlled laboratory-based, partially naturalistic laboratory and naturalistic real-world research [controlled laboratory research (CLR), partially naturalistic laboratory research approach (PNLRA), and naturalistic real-world research approach (NRWRA)]. For every category, we added one main example in brackets.

The questions are related to the specific behavior are you trying to observe and the context you aim to generalize to. If you need more precise explanation or examples, please see them for each specific question here Examples and more.
